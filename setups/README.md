# Setups

Real WorkPod Pro builds from customers and community members.

## Folders

```
photos/       Setup photos from real installs
videos/       Video walkthroughs and tours
community/    Community submitted setup guides and configs
```

## Share Your Setup

Open a Pull Request adding a folder under `community/your-name/` with:
- `photos/` — exterior and interior shots
- `setup.md` — your config, location, what you added

Or open an Issue with photos and we'll add it.

## Featured Setups

- Dual monitor + standing desk (Texas, summer-ready with mini-split)
- Streaming/gaming rig with acoustic treatment
- Legal practice office (depositions, soundproofing review in README)
- Minimalist writer's room (right-side door, north-facing)
