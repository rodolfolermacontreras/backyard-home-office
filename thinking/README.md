# Thinking

Why the WorkPod Pro exists, what we learned building it, and where it's going.

## Folders

```
research/    User research, environmental studies, remote work data
design/      Material choices, structural decisions, design tradeoffs
vision/      Long-form writing about the future of remote work and ADUs
```

## What Goes Here

- Design decision records: why we chose certain materials, dimensions, or construction methods
- Research summaries: what we learned from customers, installers, and usage data
- Essays and vision pieces about work, space, and how people use the pod

## Philosophy

The roof leans toward the light. The glass lets it in. Outside, it sits still. Inside, the day moves with you.

The WorkPod Pro is designed around one idea: the best office is the one you actually use. That means fast to install, no permits in most US cities, and a space that feels distinct from home — even when it's 20 feet away.
