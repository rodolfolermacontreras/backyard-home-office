# Bill of Materials

Complete parts list for the WorkPod Pro.

## Files

- `parts.csv` — all parts with quantities, suppliers, and part numbers
- `suppliers.md` — supplier notes and alternatives

## Categories

- Structure (panels, fasteners, seals)
- Foundation (feet, hardware)
- Electrical (cable, outlets, light, ventilator, Ethernet)
- Interior (bookshelf, door hardware)
- Optional: solar panel kit, HVAC
