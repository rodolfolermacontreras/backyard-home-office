# Suppliers

## Notes

Most structural components ship with the WorkPod Pro kit.
Customer-supplied items: outdoor electrical cable, HVAC unit.

## Customer-Supplied

| Item | Recommendation |
|------|---------------|
| Outdoor cable | 12/2 UF-B from local hardware store — length depends on your house-to-pod run |
| Portable AC | Any unit fitting max 22"L × 20"W × 88"H with 5.9" exhaust duct |
| Mini-split | Any 6,000–9,000 BTU mini-split for hot climates |

## Hardware Substitutions

- Panel bolts: M10 × 40mm stainless steel hex cap (standard hardware store)
- Foot mounting: M12 × 60mm zinc-plated (standard)
