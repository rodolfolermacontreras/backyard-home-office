# Classics

Archive of past WorkPod designs and standout community builds.

## Folders

```
designs/    Previous pod versions, concept variants, prototypes
builds/     Notable community builds with photos and notes
```

## What Goes Here

- Past product generations (WorkPod, WorkPod Mini, early prototypes)
- Community builds that solved interesting problems or showed creative configurations
- Config combinations worth preserving as reference

## Submitting a Classic Build

Open an Issue titled `[CLASSIC] your-build-name` with:
- Photos of your setup
- Location/climate context
- Anything you'd do differently
