## Installation Overview

- Foundation: place, level with 6 adjustable feet (handles up to 5° slope)
- Structure: panels click and bolt together — no special tools
- Electrical: run one outdoor cable from your home
- HVAC: portable AC or mini-split (see specs for dimensions)
- Timeline: 1–2 days for professional install, 2–3 for DIY

## HVAC Compatibility

| Type | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Portable AC | Max 22"L × 20"W × 88"H, 5.9" exhaust duct |
| Mini-split | Recommended for hot climates |
