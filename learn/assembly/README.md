# Assembly Guide

Step-by-step installation for the WorkPod Pro.

## Before You Start

- Check your local permit requirements (below threshold in most US cities)
- Identify your installation surface: grass, gravel, concrete, or slope
- Confirm your electrical drop location

## Tools Required

- Impact driver
- Level
- Tape measure
- Two people minimum

## Steps

### 1. Site Preparation
Clear and mark your 10' × 12' footprint. No excavation required.

### 2. Foundation
Place the 6 adjustable feet. Level across all axes. Torque to spec.
- See photos: `photos/step-01.jpg` through `step-04.jpg`
- Handles up to 5° slope without shimming

### 3. Floor Panel
Lower the floor panel onto the foundation feet. Confirm level.

### 4. Wall Panels
Assemble wall panels in order (see panel numbering on packaging).
- Right-side door: [view guide](https://www.autonomous.ai/assembly/right-side)
- Left-side door: [view guide](https://www.autonomous.ai/assembly/left-side)

### 5. Roof
Install roof panels. Connect the solar panel wiring if applicable.

### 6. Electrical
Run the single outdoor cable. Connect to the interior junction box.
- 3 wall outlets wired
- 1 ceiling light circuit
- 1 ventilator circuit
- 2 Ethernet ports roughed in

### 7. Interior
Install the bookshelf. Mount any HVAC unit.

### 8. Final Check
Door alignment, window seals, outlet test, ventilator test.
