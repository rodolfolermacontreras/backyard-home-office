Print this page and tick the boxes as you buy. Prices are rough El Paso estimates (2026) to
help you budget — not exact quotes.

> into plain-English shopping with **where to buy in El Paso**.

## 🧱 PART A — Structure (the "kit")

You have two paths. Pick one:

- **Path 1 — Order finished panels (easiest):** Have a **SIP (Structural Insulated Panel)**
  supplier cut panels to the CAD dimensions, including window/door openings. You just bolt them.
- **Path 2 — Build framed walls (cheaper, more work):** Standard 2×4 frame + rigid foam +
  plywood. More cutting, more skill.

### Structure shopping list
- [ ] Exterior wall panels — **8** (per BOM)
- [ ] Interior wall panels — **8**
- [ ] Roof panels — **4**
- [ ] Floor panel — **1**
- [ ] Panel connector bolts — **M10 × 40 mm stainless**, qty ~48
- [ ] Structural sealant — silicone, weatherproof — **3 tubes**
- [ ] Glass wall: **tempered glass panel(s)**, 5/16" thick (order to size from a glass shop)
- [ ] Window frame + window seals
- [ ] Door assembly + door hardware (handle/lock set)

**Where:** SIP suppliers (Premier SIPs, Extreme Panel, or a Texas fabricator — send them the
STEP files). Bolts/sealant: Home Depot, Lowe's, or Fastenal. Glass: any El Paso glass shop
(tempered **must** be cut before tempering, so give final sizes).

---

## 🦶 PART B — Foundation (no concrete!)

- [ ] Adjustable feet — **4** for Core (6 for Pro/Versatile) — screw-jack / adjustable post base
- [ ] Foot mounting bolts — **M12 × 60 mm zinc-plated**, qty ~24
- [ ] Gravel / paver pads (a solid pad under each foot) — 4 small bags of paver base + 4 patio
      pavers work well
- [ ] Landscape fabric (optional, stops weeds under the pod)

**Where:** Home Depot / Lowe's garden + hardware aisles.

---

## ⚡ PART C — Electrical (you buy, electrician installs)

- [ ] Outdoor cable — **12/2 UF-B direct-burial**, length = distance house→pod **+ 10 ft slack**
- [ ] Wall outlets — **NEMA 5-15R grounded**, qty 2 (Core) — get **GFCI** for wet-area safety
- [ ] Ceiling light — **LED panel, 12 W, ~4000K**, qty 1
- [ ] Small subpanel or weatherproof junction box + a **20A breaker**
- [ ] Outdoor-rated disconnect (electrician will advise)
- [ ] Ethernet: **Cat6 cable + 2 RJ45 keystone jacks** (optional, for fast internet)
- [ ] Conduit + fittings where cable is above ground

**Where:** Home Depot / Lowe's or an electrical-supply house. **Let your electrician confirm
the exact list for the permit.**

---

## ❄️ PART D — Comfort (El Paso essentials)

- [ ] **Mini-split AC 6,000–9,000 BTU** (best) — *or* a portable AC that fits max 22"L × 20"W × 88"H
- [ ] Weatherstripping for door
- [ ] Small dehumidifier (optional)

## 🧰 PART E — Tools

Own none? Rent the big ones from Home Depot Tool Rental or Sunbelt.

### Must-have
- [ ] Cordless **drill** + **impact driver**
- [ ] **Metric socket set** (for M10 and M12 bolts) + hex bits
- [ ] **Circular saw** or **track saw** (cutting panels/plywood)
- [ ] **Tape measure**, **pencil/marker**, **speed square**, **chalk line**
- [ ] **Level** — a 4 ft level *and* ideally a **laser level** (critical for leveling the feet)
- [ ] **Caulk gun** (for sealant)
- [ ] **Safety glasses**, **work gloves**, **dust mask**, **hearing protection**

### Nice-to-have
- [ ] **Miter saw** (clean framing cuts)
- [ ] **Jigsaw** (curved cuts / openings)
- [ ] **Torque wrench** (bolt to spec, don't overtighten)
- [ ] **Clamps**, **ladder**, **utility knife**
- [ ] **Angle grinder** with metal blade (if trimming aluminum/composite)

### Only if you DIY the wiring (else the electrician brings these)
- [ ] Wire strippers, voltage tester, fish tape

---

## ✂️ Getting materials cut to the right size

| Material | Where / how |
|----------|-------------|
| SIP / composite panels | Factory cuts them to your CAD dimensions — **best option** |
| Plywood / OSB / lumber | Home Depot & Lowe's make **free/cheap straight cuts** at the panel saw |
| Precise panels to CAD | Local cabinet/millwork shop or CNC service — send the **STEP files** |
| Tempered glass | Glass shop cuts to size **before** tempering — give exact dimensions |
| Aluminum extrusion | Buy pre-cut to length (80/20 Inc, TNUTZ, Misumi) |
